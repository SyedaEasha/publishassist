Publishing a research paper requires more than just writing. Manuscripts often need strong structure, clear formatting, and careful preparation before submission to academic journals.

PublishAssist provides support to help authors prepare their manuscripts so they are clearer, better organized, and ready for submission.

## What we help with

- Improving manuscript structure and clarity  
- Formatting according to journal requirements  
- Reviewing organization and flow  
- Preparing documents for submission readiness  

## Journal categories we support

### Scopus-oriented journals

We provide preparation support for manuscripts intended for journals aligned with Scopus indexing standards.

### X category journals

Support for manuscripts targeting recognized academic journal categories with structured preparation and formatting.

### Y category journals

Guidance for manuscripts intended for approved academic journals with appropriate structure and formatting.

## Important note

We help improve manuscript quality and submission readiness.  
Publication decisions are always made independently by journals and their reviewers.
