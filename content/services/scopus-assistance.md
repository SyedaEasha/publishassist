We provide structured assistance for authors preparing manuscripts intended for journals associated with Scopus-oriented publishing goals.

### What this service includes
- Manuscript refinement support
- Formatting and presentation checks
- Submission-readiness guidance
- Support aligned with journal expectations

### Important note
We support preparation and quality improvement, but publication decisions are always made by journals.
